Part-Time Pastry Chef / Baker

Location: Jos, Plateau State.

Job Type: Part-Time

Schedule: 3-4 shifts per week, mornings or weekends

About Us

At Omalicious Cakes & Cafe, we specialize in creating beautifully crafted pastries, cakes, and baked goods that delight our customers and celebrate the art of baking. We're passionate about flavor, creativity, and consistency — and we're looking for a dedicated, full-time pastry professional to join our growing kitchen team.

Position Summary

As a Part-Time Pastry Assistant, you’ll support our bakers with the preparation, baking, and presentation of a variety of pastries and desserts. This is a great opportunity for someone who enjoys working in a creative, fast-paced kitchen and wants to grow their skills in the baking industry.

Responsibilities

  • Assist with preparing doughs, batters, and fillings
  • Portion and shape pastries, cookies, and other baked goods
  • Help decorate and package finished products
  • Maintain a clean and organized workstation
  • Support the baking team with restocking, dishwashing, and general kitchen duties
  • Ensure consistency, freshness, and quality in all products
  • Follow food safety and hygiene standards

  • Strong knowledge of baking techniques and kitchen equipment

  • Ability to work early mornings, weekends, and holidays as needed
  • Creativity and attention to detail in both taste and presentation
  • Positive attitude, team-oriented, and a passion for the pastry arts
